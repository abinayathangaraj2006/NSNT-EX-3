EXPERIMENT 3 

 Synthesis of Tin oxide Nanoparticles by Chemical method .
 
Aim: 
  To synthesis Nanoparticle by Chemical method .

  Materials Required

        Chemical required:
        
1.      Precursor- Stannous Chloride
2.  Solvent      - Distilled water
3. Reducing agent-Sodium hydroxide
4. Surfactant    -Sodium Dodecyl Sulphate

   Apparatus required:
1.    Beaker
2.    Conical flask
3.    Measuring cylinder
4.    Magnetic stirrer
5.    Burette
6.    Thermometer
7.    pH paper

    Procedure:
1.   Dissolve tin chloride (SnCl₂ or SnCl₄) in distilled water.Stir continuously to get a clear solution.
2.   Slowly add NaOH solution (or NH₃) dropwise.Keep stirring continuously.A white precipitate of tin hydroxide will form.
3.   Maintain pH around 8–10.This ensures complete precipitation.
4.   Separate the precipitate by centrifugation or filtration.Wash with distilled water and ethanol to remove impurities.
5.   Dry the precipitate in a hot air oven at ~80–100°C for a few hours.
6.   Heat the dried powder in a furnace at 400–600°C for 2–3 hours.This converts it into SnO₂ nanoparticles.
7.   You will obtain fine white Tin Oxide (SnO₂) nanoparticle powder.
